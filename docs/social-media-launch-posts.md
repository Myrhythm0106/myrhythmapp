## Visual Guidelines

### Brand Colors
- Primary: Teal/Emerald gradient
- Accent: Gold/Amber for highlights
- Background: Soft gradients (light mode)

### Imagery Style
- Clean, accessible UI screenshots
- Warm, human photography
- Simple infographics
- Quote graphics with signature message

### Logo Usage
- Always include in corner of graphics
- Minimum clear space around logo
- Never distort or recolor
