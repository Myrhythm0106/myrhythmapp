## Founding Core scope lock (do this first)

The cohort sees **only these 9 `/launch/*` routes** plus the public funnel. Everything else is built but hidden behind the `FOUNDING_CORE_ONLY` flag. Full scope rationale and value-per-route in [`docs/founding-core-value-map.md`](./founding-core-value-map.md); full inventory in [`docs/v0.1-features.md`](./v0.1-features.md).

- [ ] Founding Core routes exposed in nav: `home`, `capture`, `commit`, `calibrate`, `memory`, `calendar`, `support`, `settings`, `profile`
- [ ] All other `/launch/*` routes hidden from nav (still reachable by direct URL is acceptable for v0.1)
- [ ] Public funnel verified: `/`, `/mvp/user-type-selection`, `/mvp/assessment`, `/mvp/payment`, `/auth`, `/privacy-policy`, `/terms`
