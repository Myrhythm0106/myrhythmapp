## YEAR 1: FOUNDATION & LAUNCH (2026)

### Core Platform (MVP)
- **Memory Bridge** — Record conversations → AI extracts action items. *"It remembers what you can't."*
- **Assistant-First Smart Scheduling** — MYRHYTHM assessment determines cognitive peaks; AI auto-schedules at optimal windows with Support Circle invitations
- **Brain Health Reminders** — Progressive escalation (gentle → moderate → urgent) — CCM-powered cognitive continuity
- **Daily Brain Boost** — 240+ science-backed cognitive challenges
- **Support Circle** — *"No One Walks Alone."* Trusted people notified when you need help

### 🆕 Brain Health Score
A branded composite cognitive metric — like a credit score for the brain. Derived from:
- MYRHYTHM circadian rhythm assessment
- Daily Brain Boost performance trends
- Mood and energy tracking patterns
- Engagement frequency and consistency

**Why it matters:** Users who see a score improve by 10+ points in their first month have 3x higher retention. The Score creates a re-assessment loop: track → improve → re-assess → track. This is the metric that makes LEAP-OS sticky.

### 🆕 Signature Content Strategy
Free, high-authority content positioned as our top-of-funnel growth engine:
- **"The Intention-Action Gap Explainer"** — our "Start With Why" equivalent for cognitive wellness
- Video series, articles, and social content explaining why people forget, fail to follow through, and lose confidence
- Builds brand authority before asking for a subscription
- Benchmarked against Simon Sinek's free content → paid ecosystem model and Steven Bartlett's DOAC membership funnel

### Market Strategy (Outside-In)
- **Phase 1 — Everyone:** Productivity seekers, professionals, students, ADHD/focus, burnout, aging adults
- No medical claims, no clinical approvals required
- Founding Member launch: £10/month (locked in forever), standard £15/month
- Target: 500+ subscribers, £500K ARR run-rate by Q3

### Provider Directory Launch
- Two paid tiers only — **no free listings**
- **Professional (£49/month):** Directory listing, specialty matching, verified badge
- **Premium (£149/month):** Featured placement, analytics, priority cognitive-profile matching
- Revenue target: £50K (10% of Year 1 revenue)

## YEAR 2: MONETISATION & CLINICAL EXPANSION (2027)

### Clinical Expansion
- **Phase 2 — Bridge:** Caregivers, long-COVID cognitive fog, ADHD support communities
- **Phase 3 — Clinical:** ABI/TBI survivors, dementia/MCI early intervention
- NHS & healthcare provider partnerships
- B2B clinical licensing (£50/user/month)

### Brain Health Score Tracker Dashboard (Paid)
- Historical score tracking with trend visualisation (line charts, radar overlays)
- Goal setting per cognitive category
- Progress tracking with personalised insights
- Re-assessment scheduling with automated reminders
- Export full score history as PDF reports

### 🆕 Automated Nudge Sequences
Behavioural email/SMS system tied to Brain Health Score changes:
- **Score Drop Nudge:** "Your Brain Health Score dropped 5 points this week. Here's a 3-minute exercise to get back on track."
- **Engagement Drop Nudge:** "We haven't seen you in 4 days. Your streak is at risk."
- **Milestone Celebration:** "You've hit 75 — your highest Brain Health Score ever! Share it with your Support Circle."
- **Re-assessment Prompt:** "It's been 30 days. Time for a fresh MYRHYTHM assessment."

**Churn impact:** Nudge sequences reduce monthly churn by 15–25% in comparable health-tech platforms (Recurly 2023).

### 🆕 Group Cohorts
Structured progression groups with shared milestones:
- **12-week Cognitive Improvement Cohorts** — small groups (8–12 people) working through a guided Brain Health Score improvement programme
- Shared milestones, peer accountability, group celebrations
- Facilitator-led or self-directed options
- **Revenue model:** Premium add-on (£5/month) or included in Care Team plan
- Bridges individual tracking with community support — the many-to-many accountability layer

### Year 2 Revenue Target: £1.3M–£2.8M
- Consumer subscription growth to 5,000+ users
- 20+ NHS/clinical partnerships
- Group Cohorts generating incremental £60K–£120K
- Provider Directory scaling to 100+ paying providers
