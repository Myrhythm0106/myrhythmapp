## Executive Summary

MyRhythm is a cognitive empowerment platform designed for individuals with memory challenges, TBI survivors, caregivers, and anyone seeking to build better brain health habits. This document outlines our phased launch strategy from Founding Member through Scale.

### Core Value Proposition
**"Transform conversations into kept promises"**

The only reminder app built on brain science, featuring:
- 🎙️ Memory Bridge - Record & extract action items
- 🧠 Brain Health Reminders - Progressive escalation system
- 💪 Daily Brain Boost - 240+ science-backed challenges
- 💚 Support Circle - Your personal safety net

## Pricing Strategy

### ✅ SIMPLIFIED: Single Tier Pricing

| Period | Founding Member | Regular Price | Your Savings |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|--------------|
| Monthly | **£10/month** | £15/month | £5/month forever |
| Annual | **£100/year** | £150/year | £50/year forever |

**Why One Tier?**
- Eliminates decision fatigue for users
- Simplifies marketing and support
- Everyone gets the full experience
- Reduces user confusion
- Can introduce tiers later based on data

### Price Increase Triggers
Price will increase from £10 → £15/month when ANY of these conditions are met:
1. **User Milestone:** 500 paying subscribers
2. **Time-Based:** 6 months after launch (June 2025)
3. **Feature Milestone:** 90%+ of planned v1.0 features complete

**Founding Members keep their price FOREVER** - this is the key marketing message.

## Feature Rollout Strategy

### The "What's New" Approach

Instead of saving features for new tiers, we:
1. **Release features to ALL users** via the What's New page
2. **Communicate value** through in-app notifications
3. **Reduce support burden** with clear documentation
4. **Build loyalty** by continuously adding value

### Feature Release Cadence
- **Monthly:** Bug fixes, minor improvements
- **Quarterly:** Major feature releases
- **Annually:** Platform evolution (v1.0, v2.0)

### Communication Strategy
1. In-app "What's New" page (notification badge)
2. Email announcement to subscribers
3. Social media celebration
4. Founder video for major releases

## Appendix A: Archived Strategies

### v1.0 Strategy (Superseded)
The original three-tier pricing (MyStarter £7, MyStretch £13, MyLeap £20) was simplified to a single tier based on:
- User feedback indicating decision paralysis
- Complexity in marketing messaging
- Support overhead with tier questions
- Desire to maximize feature access for all users
