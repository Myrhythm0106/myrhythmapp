# Memory Bridge — Hero Feature Positioning Decision

**Status:** Awaiting founder sign-off
**Owner:** Marketing / brand
**Date raised:** 27 May 2026

## Proposal
Lead all public-facing marketing (landing page, app store listing, founding-member pitch) with **Memory Bridge** as the named hero feature, in place of (or above) the 4Cs loop.

## Rationale
- Memory Bridge is the single feature competitors cannot replicate without rebuilding the support-circle + clinical-export stack.
- It is the most demonstrable "Clinical-Ready vs Life-Ready Gap" proof point in under 10 seconds (record → review → share-with-clinician PDF).
- The 4Cs loop is a powerful internal operating model but reads as abstract to first-time visitors.

## What this is *not*
- Not a change to the in-app 4Cs flow.
- Not a rename of any feature.
- Not an engineering task — purely a positioning and copy decision.

## Decision needed
Approve / reject leading external messaging with Memory Bridge. If approved, marketing copy on landing + pricing + onboarding will be rewritten in a follow-up pass.
